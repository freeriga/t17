Turgeņeva iela 17

neighborhood residence

The T17 residence, a Free Riga project, is an old wooden house near the Academy of Sciences in the "Lastādija" neighborhood of central Riga.

Residents organize things for the neighborhood and city. This site is a hub for such projects.

Apply for T17 residency starting January 2018.
